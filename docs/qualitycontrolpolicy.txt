Quality Control Policy
**********************

After a code file is completed it is to be reviewed during an Inspection Meeting.

Inspection Meeting Format:
	Minimum of 4 people in attendance, the author must be in attendance.
	A person other than the author is the guide the participants through the material.
	Both the code and the associated documentation constitute the material to be reviewed.
	All changes that are agreed upon by a majority of the meeting participants is to be logged in the meeting minutes and be completed by the original author.
	If changes are requested, a follow-up Inspection Meeting is to organized.
	During a follow-up Inspection Meeting, confirmation that requested changes were completed is to be logged in the meeting minutes.
